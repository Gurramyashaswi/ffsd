Seller Prerequisites

The prerequistes for seller are:-

  • User has to first sign up for displaying his works in our web application.
  • For sign up he has to go to "Artist" option available in our Home page.
  • After Signing up, then he can uplaod his/her works in our website.
  • Then the user(We consider him as our seller) has to wait for our approval of his works. Once, his works were approved by us, then his works will be displayed in our website.
  • The seller can check the website to check the price fo the works, or he can check the feedback left by the buyers.


Seller / Buyer log in or sign up

When the users click on profile option, Then the user can see that there are two options. They are:

  1. Sellers account.
  2. Buyers account
  • In sellers account, There is only one option i.e., login. Because, the artist/seller's account will be created when the artist/seller displays his works at the column artist.
  • In buyers account, There are two options i.e., login or sign up. If a user visits our website for the first time then he/she can click on Sign-up option. Then we will ask the user to enter a username, email-id and a password. Then the user will be directed into the home page of our web application. Then the user can search the artworks by artistname.


Purchasing Instructions

  • If a user wants to buy an artwork or group of artworks then the user can click directly "Order now" for one work.But if the user wants to purchase group of artworks then there is an option available i.e., "Add to Cart" where user can add all the items he/she wants to purchase.
  • After selecting the works then the user can click "Order now" and then we ask the details of your address.
  • After submitting your address, then we will ask about your payment options.
The available options are:
  1. Google Pay
  2. Phone Pay
  3. Cash On Delivery
  4. Debit Card
  5. Credit Card
  • For cash on delivery option, We will confirm your order and then we will provide you the estimated delivery date.
  • For the remaining payment options, we will direct the page to their respective pages where the transaction takes place and then we provide the estimated delivery date.


Manage Address

Here you can add as many addresses as you want to deliver the products. We will use google maps to locate the area to deliver the artworks.


Payment settings

Here users can check their default preferences of their payment type. Or they can check their saved debit/credit cards.


Account Settings

Here the user can update his username, password or email-id.
